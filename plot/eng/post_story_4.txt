 They are strange. I came to eat, and they checked me 
with a metal detector.
 They asked me to show my pockets. What are the pockets? 
I'm the most ordinary cat! But despite this, I was able 
to solve the problems that were given to me. They were
pretty easy. In my box I already knew how to solve it.
 In fact, I liked that big strange book that lived with
my master. When he opened it and went for tea, I used
to like to sleep on it. It was very warm and pleasant.
But the master drove me away every time.
 Therefore, I had to sit next to and watch as he
cleverly presses the buttons of the book...
